Single CTA
Guide travelers toward one clear action.
What is the single CTA?
In Navify, the single call-to-action is to start navigation: set a destination, choose a mode, and follow the route together with your Trusted Circle.
Where is it placed?
The inputs for location and destination, the Drive/Walk mode selector, and the Guide button live together around the map so users are pulled directly into the navigation experience without digging through menus.
