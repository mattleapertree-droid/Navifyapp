Navigator Guider
The Navigator Guider idea is captured by Navify's Guide mode: tap on the map, set a destination, and the app draws a road-following route with simulated traffic colors and an ETA. When Guide is on, the route stands out so you can follow it like a simple navigator while your Trusted Circle keeps an eye on your trip.
