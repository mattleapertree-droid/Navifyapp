Profile Settings
All your saved Navify data in one place.
What lives here?
This page represents where Navify stores your profile settings, favorites, likes, saved items, and other lists so they are easy to access when you travel.
Examples
  • Saved destinations like home, school, work, or favorite cafes.
  • Favorite routes for driving or walking that you reuse often.
  • Trusted Circle lists of friends and family who can see your status.
  • Personal preferences such as your display name and avatar.
On the main Navify app, these settings are powered by local storage and modals like the avatar picker and name preferences.
