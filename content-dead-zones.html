Content Dead Zones
Hidden but still important navigation items.
What are content dead zones?
In Navify, content dead zones are secondary navigation items that you do not see on the main home screen but are still valuable to your experience.
Examples in Navify
  • Information pages like this one, accessed from the hamburger menu.
  • Help or support sections that you only open when needed.
  • Detailed design notes about how Trusted Circle or Guide mode works.
  • Advanced settings that are not required for a normal trip.
The goal is to keep the main map simple while still giving power users a path to deeper content when they want it.
